Subha

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 July 2026

Subha is designed to be private by default. Dhikr counts and app preferences stay on your device. The app does not require an account.

Information Stored On Your Device

Subha may store the following on your device:

  • Dhikr counts
  • Streaks and milestones
  • Display name
  • Language and theme preferences
  • Reading preferences
  • Reminder preferences

This information is stored locally so the app can show your progress and remember your settings.

Sharing

Sharing is optional. If you choose to share a milestone card, Subha opens your device's system share sheet. You decide where to send it.

Photos Permission

Subha asks for photo-library permission only when you choose to save a milestone card as an image. The permission is used to save that image to your photo library.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Subha schedules local notifications on your device for dhikr reminders. Reminder content is stored and scheduled on the device.

Purchases

Subha Pro purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. Subha does not receive your payment-card details.

RevenueCat is used to validate receipts, restore purchases, and determine whether Pro access is active. RevenueCat may process an anonymous app user identifier, device and operating-system details, store receipt or purchase token, purchase history, entitlement status, and the time the app was last seen. This information is used for app functionality, purchase analytics, fraud prevention, and customer support.

Subha does not send your display name or dhikr history to RevenueCat. Advertising identifier collection and RevenueCat diagnostics are disabled in the app.

Data Deletion

You can remove locally stored counts and preferences using the reset options in Subha or by deleting the app. To request deletion of purchase-related data processed through RevenueCat, contact zaxskylab@gmail.com. Purchase records may also remain with Apple or Google as required by their terms and applicable law.

Analytics And Tracking

The current build does not include behavioral analytics, advertising analytics, or cross-app tracking. RevenueCat processes purchase and entitlement events as described above.

If analytics or crash reporting are added later, this policy will be updated before release.

Religious Content

Subha includes source-checked dhikr content, but it is not a replacement for qualified religious guidance.
