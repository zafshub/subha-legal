Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 July 2026

Subha is designed to be private by default. In the current build, dhikr counts and app preferences stay on your device. The app does not require an account.

Information Stored On Your Device

Subha may store the following on your device:

  • Dhikr counts
  • Streaks and milestones
  • Display name
  • Language and theme preferences
  • Reading preferences
  • Reminder preferences

This information is stored locally so the app can show your progress and remember your settings.

Sharing

Sharing is optional. If you choose to share a milestone card, Subha opens your device's system share sheet. You decide where to send it.

Photos Permission

Subha asks for photo-library permission only when you choose to save a milestone card as an image. The permission is used to save that image to your photo library.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Subha schedules local notifications on your device for dhikr reminders. Reminder content is stored and scheduled on the device.

Purchases

Subha Pro is prepared for future App Store and Google Play checkout. When Pro goes live, purchases and restore checks will be handled by the relevant store payment system and may use a purchase provider such as RevenueCat to unlock Pro access.

If this changes, this Privacy Policy and the App Store / Google Play privacy disclosures must be updated before release.

Analytics And Tracking

The current build does not include analytics tracking and does not track users across apps or websites.

If analytics or crash reporting are added later, this policy must be updated before release.

Religious Content

Subha includes source-checked dhikr content, but it is not a replacement for qualified religious guidance.
