Privacy Policy - Effective July 19, 2026

Information we collect

The Last Broadcast does not require an account and does not intentionally collect names, email addresses, contacts, precise location, advertising identifiers, or gameplay analytics.

Game saves

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. They are not used for advertising and are not sold to third parties.

The app connects to secure hosting to load game content. The hosting provider may process basic technical request information, such as an IP address, device or browser information, and timestamps, as needed to deliver and protect the service.

Children

The game is not directed to young children. It contains suspense, fear themes, and references to danger and death.
